01 — THE BOTTLENECK

What regulatory writing teams are up against

Every NDA/BLA submission is a race against a document backlog that grows with every trial readout.

PAIN 01
4–6 months
Manual drafting cycles
Writing a CSR, refreshing an IB, or assembling NDA modules 2.5–2.7 from scratch typically runs four to six months per document, start to sign-off.
PAIN 02
3,000+ pages
Data volume per submission
Writers work through listings, tables, safety narratives, and protocol amendments by hand to find the numbers that belong in each section.
PAIN 03
$2–3M+ delay cost
Consistency errors & delay risk
A mismatched adverse-event count between CSR and IB, caught late, can trigger a query cycle — and submission delays scale into the billions industry-wide.
